Chicken Biryani

Home


Description

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant, layered South Asian rice dish featuring marinated chicken, long-grain basmati rice,aromatic spices (like cardamom, cloves, saffron), herbs (mint, cilantro), and fried onions, traditionally slow-cooked together in a sealed pot (dum pukht) to infuse flavors and create tender meat and fluffy rice, resulting in a vibrant, flavorful main course.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • green Chillies
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Fresh mint
  • Cooking oil

Steps to cook

  1. Marinate the Chicken
    1. Mix chicken with yogurt leaves, spices
    2. Add chopped mint and coriander
    3. Marinate for at least 1 hour
  2. Cook the rice
    1. Wash basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes.
    2. Boil water with salt, bay leaf, cloves, cardamom.
    3. Add rice and cook till 70-80% done.
    4. Drain and keep aside.
  3. Prepare the Chicken Masala
    1. Heat oil in heavy pan
    2. Fry sliced onions till golden brown.
    3. Add green chillies and tomatoes
    4. Add marinated chicken and cook on medium flame till chicken is 80% cooked and oil separates.
  4. Layer the Biryani
    1. Lower the flame
    2. Spread the chicken evenly
    3. Add a layer of rice
    4. repeat layers while sprinkling onions, Mint, coriander, Saffron milk
  5. Serve
